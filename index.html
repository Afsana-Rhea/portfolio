About Me

My name is Afsana Ria Miji and I grew up in Queens. I am eldest of 3 and am trying to be someone who is more giving and reliable. I want to make sure that the people I come in contact with feel seen, and are seen, though I am careless at times. The jobs that I have had are, nanny, au pair, early education english teacher, insurance sales person, office manager, receptionist, sales person, yoga instructor, writer, online store owner, social media marketer and very soon to be web developer.

I am a 28 year old devotee of life. I live to connect with my surroundings and overcome anything that does not serve me.
